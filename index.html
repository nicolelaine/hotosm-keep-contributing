Every map traced, every community linked, and every life touched - your impact counts!

At HOT, we believe in the power of maps to change lives. By participating in our mapathon, you're not just adding data to a map - you're contributing to real-world impact. Your support enables us to provide vital resources to communities in need, from disaster relief efforts to healthcare accessibility projects.

Ready to Put Your Pin on the Map?

After experiencing the impact of mapping firsthand, we invite you to join us in our mission.

By becoming a donor, you will help us continue to support initiatives worldwide, making a lasting difference in the lives of countless individuals by helping communities add local data on top of the base maps that you have helped create today. Local communities will then be able to use that data to create local and sustainable solutions.

Your ongoing support allows us to sustain our mapping initiatives and expand our reach to even more communities in need. Together, we can create a world where everyone has access to accurate and up-to-date maps!
